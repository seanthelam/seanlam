About Me

Third-Year Undergraduate Student

I'm an undergraduate student passionate about the convergence of physics, chemistry, materials science, and computational science. My research interests span in chemical physics and the development of novel algorithms to understand complex systems within quantum and energy materials. I am also interested in pursuing graduate school and academia.

Outside of academics, I am a track and field athlete, specialized in long sprints, 400m hurdles, and triple jump. I am the President of the Society of Physics Students Chapter at Colorado College. I am a mental health advocate through Colorado College's Morgan's Message Chapter. I volunteer as a multimedia content creator for WITHUS, a clothing brand I helped lay the foundations for.
